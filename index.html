About

I am a graduate student working on the ATLAS Experiment at CERN, affiliated with the Université de Montréal under the supervision of Prof. Jean-François Arguin. My research focuses on ML-based anomaly detection for searches for physics beyond the Standard Model in proton–proton collision data collected by the ATLAS detector at the Large Hadron Collider, as well as detector hardware development for the ATLAS Inner Tracker upgrade.

Outside of physics I have over a decade of experience in music recording and film production. My short films have received Official Selection, Finalist, and Preselection distinctions at international film festivals, and I hold an Award of Merit from the IndieFest Film Awards.
